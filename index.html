Mi Trabajo

FE + CULTURA Podcast

Founded in 1993, the Christ For The Nations Association of Bible Schools (CFNABS) is committed to advancing the expansion of God's Kingdom across nations through the enhancement of leadership training in affiliated Bible schools. One such example is Christ For The Nations Colombia or Cristo Para Las Naciones Colombia (CPN). Under the guidance of devoted founders and directors Jerry and Barbara Manderfield, and with the support of a compassionate community, the students at CFNI Colombia are experiencing the discovery of healing, hope, and a profound sense of purpose in their lives. We invite you to watch Part I of this documentary series to learn more about our international ministries.
